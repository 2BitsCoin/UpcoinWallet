NEW ACCOUNT - NOTICE

This feature involves the creation of a new account on Waves platform.

The procedure consists of complex cryptographic operations that use a SEED
(a series of random words) to create a unique address for your account.

This SEED is extremely important and must be kept in safe place, since
it's the only way to recover your account in case of lost, corrupted data
or hardware failure.

Your SEED will also allow you to IMPORT your account to other devices.
